Ethical Sourcing

At Marilyn Jean Hair Boutique, we are committed to ethical sourcing practices. Our raw hair extensions are sourced from single donors who willingly and consciously choose to sell their hair. This ensures that the sourcing process is ethical, respectful, and fair. We believe in transparency and traceability, providing you with the confidence that your beauty choices support ethical and sustainable practices.
