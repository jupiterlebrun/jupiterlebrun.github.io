Makeover for a Cause Foundation

The "Makeover for a Cause" Foundation, established by Marilyn Jean Hair Boutique, aims to uplift and empower individuals in need by providing them with professional makeovers that boost their confidence and self-esteem. We believe that everyone deserves to feel beautiful and confident, regardless of their circumstances.

Objectives

  • Identify individuals in need who would benefit from a professional makeover.
  • Provide high-quality hair services and products, generously donated by Marilyn Jean Hair Boutique.
  • Collaborate with local makeup artists, stylists, and photographers to create a comprehensive makeover experience.
  • Share inspiring stories of transformation and personal growth, celebrating the positive impact of the makeovers.

    • Selection Process

    Application

    Interested individuals or their friends/family can submit a brief application, describing the applicant's story, challenges they've faced, and why they would benefit from a makeover.

    Review

    A dedicated team of Marilyn Jean Hair Boutique staff and volunteers will review the applications and select candidates based on their needs and potential impact.

    Notification

    Selected candidates will be contacted and invited to participate in the "Makeover for a Cause" program.

    Community Involvement

    The Makeover for a Cause Foundation encourages community involvement by inviting local businesses and volunteers to contribute their time, expertise, and resources to the program. This collaborative approach not only maximizes the positive impact on the selected individuals but also fosters a sense of community and shared purpose.

    Success Stories

    The foundation will share inspiring stories of transformation on Marilyn Jean Hair Boutique's website and social media channels, showcasing the positive impact of the makeovers and celebrating the resilience and strength of the participants. These stories will serve as a reminder that beauty, confidence, and personal growth are attainable for everyone, regardless of their circumstances.
